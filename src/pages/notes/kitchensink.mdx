## Math

Inline: $x^2 + y^2 = z^2$

Block:

$$
\begin{aligned}
  x^2 + y^2 &= z^2 \\
  x^2 &= z^2 - y^2 \\
  x &= \sqrt{z^2 - y^2}
\end{aligned}
$$
